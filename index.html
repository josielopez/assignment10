Take your photography to the next level by shooting in black and white.

Below are four examples of using jQuery to illustrate the difference color, or lack of color, can make in a photo.

Mouse over the images to compare the effects, or, if you are using a mobile device, use the blue buttons to apply the effects.

1. Color can help highlight your subject.

Most noticeably in colorful subjects. Check it out below.

Photo by David Clode on Unsplash

2. Greyscale can change the mood of your photo.

A vibrant sunset becomes an interesting river of air coursing through plumes of smoke.

Photo by NON on Unsplash

3. You can also use it to mute the subject in your photo.

Red and green are colors that are similar in shade and tone. Some color-blind individuals are unable to distinguish the two -- mouse over the image below to understand why.

Photo by Jairu Ollennu on Unsplash

4. Black and white photos can make an image feel more abstract.
